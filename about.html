About Biakone

Visual Artist — Designer
↘ Urban culture, graffiti & scale work

Biakone —or Biako— is my visual alter ego.

It started as my graffiti name and has grown into a visual lab where I mix urban culture, design, physical objects, miniatures, stickers, 3D printing, painting and digital experiments.

A space to build pieces, test ideas and reinterpret elements.

Some pieces become scale scenes; others become objects, tags, art toys, posters, interactive tools or simple visual studies.

An ongoing archive: design, objects, symbols and urban codes reinterpreted from the studio.
